project description

Set on a sprawling 4 bigha expanse in the enchanting locale of Samod, Rajasthan, this resort is a masterful blend of tranquillity and luxury, seamlessly merging the indoor with the outdoor. Conceived as an oasis, it invites guests into a world where nature's beauty and top-tier amenities coexist harmoniously. Every detail of this architectural marvel is designed to provide an immersive experience, where one can revel in the scenic splendour while enjoying a host of activities and luxuries. The layout of the resort is a harmonious mix of open spaces and intimate nooks, creating a sanctuary that offers both relaxation and entertainment. At its heart is a stunning swimming pool, an inviting oasis surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for leisurely dips under the Rajasthani sun. The pool area, with its serene water and verdant surroundings, serves as a focal point, offering a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Adjacent to the pool is a sprawling garden, meticulously landscaped to host grand parties, weddings, and other celebratory events. This verdant backdrop enhances every occasion, making it memorable and picturesque. The resort boasts an exquisite restaurant and a chic bar, each designed to provide a unique culinary journey. The restaurant, with its elegant decor and ambient lighting, offers both indoor and outdoor seating options. Guests can choose to dine under the stars, enjoying the cool evening breeze, or in a stylish, air-conditioned setting. The menu is a delightful blend of local and international cuisines, crafted to satisfy even the most discerning palate. The bar, with its contemporary design and cozy ambiance, is an ideal spot for unwinding with a cocktail. The selection of drinks, from classic favourite to innovative mixes, ensures that there is something for everyone. Accommodation at the resort is the epitome of luxury, with spacious, well-appointed bedrooms that offer breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. Each room is designed with comfort and elegance in mind, featuring modern amenities and plush furnishings to ensure a restful stay. The decor is a tasteful blend of traditional Rajasthani elements and contemporary design, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere. Large windows and private balconies allow guests to soak in the beauty of the landscape, making every moment spent in the room a serene experience. The entire resort is enveloped in greenery, with pathways meandering through lush gardens and shaded by tall trees. These pathways lead to various nooks and corners of the resort, each offering a unique perspective and a moment of tranquillity. The gardens, with their vibrant flowers and carefully curated plants, create a serene and picturesque environment. This seamless integration of indoor and outdoor spaces not only enhances the aesthetic appeal but also promotes a sense of peace and well-being. Guests can enjoy a leisurely stroll through the gardens, relax under the shade of a tree, or simply sit and admire the natural beauty around them. The resort also offers a range of activities designed to cater to all age groups and interests. From yoga sessions and spa treatments to adventure sports and cultural performances, there is something for everyone. The staff, with their warm hospitality and attention to detail, ensure that every guest feels welcome and well cared for.
