TOWNSHIP

Jaipur, rajasthan

Project details

  • CLIENT :CAHRT GROUP
  • TYPE :TOWNSHIP
  • BUILT UP AREA :25,000 SQFT
  • SITE AREA :45 BIGHA
  • STATUS :Ongoing

project description

Located on a sprawling 45 bigha expanse in Jaipur, CAHRT Township is a visionary project designed to offer residents an aesthetic and serene living experience. The township masterfully blends angles and curves to create a visually captivating environment that exudes peace and tranquillity. At the heart of CAHRT Township lies its architectural brilliance, where every structure is thoughtfully crafted to embody tranquillity and beauty. The use of angles and curves is not merely for visual appeal but to create a sense of flow and harmony throughout the township. This design philosophy is prominently showcased in the mandir space, a serene retreat for spiritual reflection. Its gentle curves and strategic angles evoke a feeling of calm and reverence, making it a perfect sanctuary for meditation and prayer. The township is enveloped in a lush green blanket, with meticulously planned softscapes and landscapes that enhance the aesthetic appeal while promoting a sense of well-being. Verdant gardens, vibrant flower beds, and towering trees form a natural tapestry that delights the senses. The pathways winding through these gardens invite residents to take leisurely strolls, immersing themselves in nature's beauty. The landscape design incorporates a variety of plant species to ensure a palette of colours and textures throughout the year, making every walk a new experience. CAHRT Township is designed with the community in mind, offering a plethora of recreational spaces that cater to all age groups. The children's play area is a haven of joy and safety, where the little ones can explore and play to their heart's content. For adults, there are numerous serene spots and leisure areas where they can relax, socialize, or simply enjoy the peaceful surroundings. One of the standout features of the township is the unique chess maze. This innovative space is designed to engage both adults and children, providing a fun and intellectually stimulating environment. The chess maze is not only a place for strategic play but also a community hub where residents can gather and interact. Surrounded by beautiful flower planters and gardens, it serves as a focal point of the township, enhancing its communal spirit. The serene mandir space is a tranquil haven designed to offer a peaceful retreat for spiritual reflection and worship. Adding to the township's charm are the elegant pergolas, a structure that epitomizes peace and beauty through its design. The pergola's graceful curves and sturdy angles create a perfect blend of form and function, offering a shaded retreat where residents can unwind. The surrounding gardens, with their riot of colours and scents, make this spot a sensory delight, ideal for quiet reflection or casual gatherings. The abundant greenery, the thoughtfully designed structures, and the seamless integration of angles and curves all contribute to a harmonious environment. The township is covered with beautiful trees and flowers, creating a picturesque setting that uplifts the spirit and promotes a sense of well-being. There is a serene mandir space, offering a peaceful retreat for spiritual reflection and worship.
